The Rules Of The Game

General:

Amount of players: 2-10

How to play?

Each turn, a player is assigned to be the artist, and the others are guessers.
The artist is prompted with 3 options, of which he picks one and has to draw it in the time limit.
To do so, the artist may draw strokes, fill shapes, change colors and the width of the pen.
He also has two buttons, one to clear the board and one to undo his last action.

The guesser need to guess what is he drawing, by typing in the chat before the turn ends.
They can see at the top of the screen the amount of letters the secret word has, and throughout the turn, as time runs, some letters will reveal themselves.
If the difficulty is set to Difficult, the letters won't reveal their place in the word.
If a player correctly guesses the word, he receives points and can no longer send messages to the players who haven't yet correctly guess.
He may still send messages to people who did find the word and to the artist.
These points are seen in the scoreboard on the left side of the screen.
A guesser can buy a hint, costing some points, in order to see another letter. This option is limited to once per turn.
There's two types of hints:

  • Private: only the player who bought it can see the extra letter, however it costs more.
  • Public: everyone sees the extra letter. It's cheaper, but also denies everyone of buying another hint.

How do you win?

It depends on the game mode:

  • Classic: the game ends at the last round (the amount of rounds is set by the lobby's admin) and the person with the most points wins.
  • Points Rush: the first player to reach the points limit (is also set by the lobby's admin) wins.

What to do if a player plays inappropriately?

The admin (creator of the room) may kick any player he wishes to, as long as there are more than 2 players.
