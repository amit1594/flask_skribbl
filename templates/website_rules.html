The Rules Of The Website


Cookies

This website is using cookies as a media to store your username.

Appropriate Behaviour

You may not, under no circumstances, send hateful or spiteful comments to other players.
You may not use malicious usernames.
You may not draw any inappropriate or rude drawings.
Respect others, be kind. Remember that you should treat others the way you do yourself.
As an admin, do not kick players at your will. Only do so if the player is ruining the game experience for others.


Play Fair

Do not use misuse features such as playing with friends to negatively impact other players.
You must draw the chosen word during you turn as an artist, and must try guessing the word while being a guesser.
Try your hardest! Don't waste people's time.


Have Fun
